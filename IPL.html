The IPL History

The IPL logo was designed by a UK-based agency called ‘UK-based VentureThree’ back in 2008. The logo is an amalgamation of several elements, including the lion, the crown, the trophy, and the batsman. The logo is a perfect representation of the IPL’s vision, which is to bring the best of cricketing talent from around the world onto a single stage.
